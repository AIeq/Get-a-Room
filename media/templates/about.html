Got group work? Get-a-Room

Get-a-Room is a booking system for reserving common spaces in the university.
  • How to reserve?
    1. Start by selecting the building
    2. Enter your email
    3. Select time & date
    4. Click on "Reserve"
  • How to cancel?
    1. Enter your email
    2. Click on "Manage reservations"
    3. Cancel or rebook using the links in the email we sent you :)
